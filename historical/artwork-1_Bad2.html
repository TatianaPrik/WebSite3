Ethereal Convergence

Medium: Oil on Canvas

Dimensions: 36" √ó 48"

Year: 2024

Price: $3,200

This piece explores the intersection of consciousness and the digital realm, where organic forms merge with geometric precision. The flowing blues and ethereal whites represent the fluidity of thought, while the structured elements symbolize our increasingly digital existence.

Created during a period of deep introspection about humanity's relationship with technology, this work invites viewers to contemplate the boundaries between the natural and artificial, the conscious and the programmed.
